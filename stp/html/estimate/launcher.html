Algorithm

Imagine a rectangle of height h, width b - a, and area A = h(b - a) such that the function f(x) is within the boundaries of the rectangle. Generate n pairs of random numbers xi and yi with a ≤ xi ≤ b and 0 ≤ yi ≤ h. The fraction of points xi, yi that satisfy the condition yi ≤ f(xi) is an estimate of the ratio of the integral of f(x) to the area of the rectangle.
