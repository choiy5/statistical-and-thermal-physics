Hard disks

Introduction

How can we understand the temperature and density dependence of the equation of state and the structure of a dense liquid? One way to gain more insight into this dependence is to modify the interaction and see how the properties of the system change. In particular, we would like to understand the relative role of the repulsive and attractive parts of the interaction. Simulations of an idealized system of hard disks (and hard spheres) have played an important role in the development of our understanding of simple liquids.
